Step 1: Setup Connection

Go to Settings and choose how you connect to RoleTect:

Option A: Localhost

If RoleTect is running on this computer.

Port: 14201
Option B: Custom Local IP

If RoleTect is on a different PC in your house.

IP: 192.168.1.5 | Port: 14201
Option C: Remote / Cloud

If using a website/server URL.

URL: https://my-backend.com

* Do not include "/ingest" in the URL.

Step 2: Authenticate

Paste your Secret Key from the RoleTect App (found in the Inbox tab or Settings) into the Secret Key field and click Save.

Step 3: Ingest Data

Navigate to a job post, go to the Extract tab, and click Send to RoleTect. The Selector tells the extension which part of the page to copy (default is everything).
