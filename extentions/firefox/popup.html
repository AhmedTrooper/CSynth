How it works
1
Navigate

Open any job listing page (LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.) in your browser.

2
Capture

Switch to the Extract tab. Use the default selector or target a specific area.

3
Ingest

Click "Send to RoleTect". Your job is saved to the Inbox vault for AI analysis.

Ensure your RoleTect instance (Desktop or Cloud) is reachable and your Secret Key is set in the Settings tab.
